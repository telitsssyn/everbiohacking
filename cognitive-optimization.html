Cognitive optimization

Goal: To enhance learning capacity and specific cognitive metrics, such as processing speed, memory, focus, and others.

Learning

Study complex systems: languages, mathematics, physics, programming, or music. It is crucial, however, to have a genuine interest in these subjects; do not force yourself.

Application of Knowledge

Knowledge that is not applied is simply forgotten—this is an unavoidable fact. Practice languages, apply mathematics to real-life scenarios, and solve life's challenges using your accumulated knowledge.

The Grind

Beyond innate talent and luck, what distinguishes a genius from an outsider is the amount of deliberate practice. The "grind" is the great equalizer. It is the opportunity to surpass anyone in any field. General intelligence and cognitive abilities are sharpened only through consistent, hard work.

Supplements

My preferred supplements:

  • Lion’s Mane (micelio): Stimulates Nerve Growth Factor (NGF) and enhances learning.
  • Bacopa Monnieri: Improves synaptic transmission and memory while promoting neuronal growth. It enhances learning capacity.
  • Citicoline: A precursor to acetylcholine that strengthens neuronal membranes and boosts the brain's energy potential.
  • Caffeine + L-Theanine: A classic combination to achieve a state of "calm alertness," where theanine neutralizes caffeine-induced anxiety while maintaining focus.
